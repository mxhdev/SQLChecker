!3 Time

|Execute|Create table datatypetest(id int, my_time time)|

|Ordered Query|select cast(sysdatetime() as time) my_time|
|my_time                                                 |
|>>tm1                                                   |

|insert|datatypetest|
|id    |my_time     |
|1     |<<tm1       |

|ordered query|select * from datatypetest|
|id           |my_time                   |
|1            |<<tm1                     |

|ordered query|select * from datatypetest|
|id           |my_time?                  |
|1            |<<tm1                     |

!3 Millisecond times

|execute|delete from datatypetest|

|insert|datatypetest|
|id    |my_time     |
|1     |11:12:22.1  |

|query|select * from datatypetest|
|id   |my_time?                  |
|1    |11:12:22.1                |

|query|select * from datatypetest|
|id   |my_time?                  |
|1    |fail[11:12:22.2]          |

!3 should accept fail[null]
|query|select * from datatypetest|
|id   |my_time?                  |
|1    |fail[null]                |
